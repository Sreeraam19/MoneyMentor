🙋‍♂️ Frequently Asked Questions

Is my financial data safe?

Absolutely. MoneyMentor uses Django’s secure authentication system and encrypted database practices to ensure only you can see your numbers.

Can I use MoneyMentor on my phone?

Yes! The interface is built with responsive design, meaning it adjusts perfectly to your phone, tablet, or laptop screen.

Does the app connect to my bank account?

For maximum privacy, MoneyMentor is a manual tracker. You enter your transactions yourself, giving you full control and awareness of every rupee.

Can I create my own spending categories?

Yes. While we provide defaults like "Food" and "Transport," you can head to Settings to add custom categories like "Gaming" or "Hobby."

How do the charts help me?

Charts turn boring numbers into a story. They help you spot "hidden leaks" in your budget — like realizing you spend 40% of your income on snacks!

Is MoneyMentor free to use?

Yes, the core tracking and analytics features are completely free for all users as part of our mission to build financial literacy.

What happens if I forget to log an expense?

No problem. You can back-date transactions to any day in the past to ensure your monthly report stays accurate.

Can I export my data?

We are working on a feature that will allow you to download your full transaction history as a CSV file for use in Excel or Google Sheets.
